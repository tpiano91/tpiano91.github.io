Welcome to my portfolio

My name is Tristan and I'm an American data analyst based in Toronto. I am also a freelance classical musician with a doctorate in performance and over a decade of experience as a teacher, collaborative pianist and perfromer. For the past several years, I've also worked as an adjudicator for piano competitions/festivals and have given numerous public masterclasses. In 2024, I completed the Data Analytics Course at CareerFoundry, including a specialization course in Data Visualizations with Python. My journey revealed significant overlap between the technical demands of data analysis and my existing skill set - including attention to detail, curiosity, analytical thinking, communication, and organization. I am eager to continue honing these skills and developing new skills in my new position.
