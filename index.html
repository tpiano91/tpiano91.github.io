American Data Analyst in Toronto

In 2024, I completed the Data Analytics Course at CareerFoundry, including a specialization course in Data Visualizations with Python. My journey revealed significant overlap between the technical demands of data analysis my existing skill set - including attention to detail, curiosity, analytical thinking, communication and organization. I am eager to continue honing these skills in a new analyst role, available for for work in Toronto, or remote work.
